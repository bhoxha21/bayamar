About

Explore the Albanian Riviera

Explore the breathtaking Albanian Riviera with Bayamar Adventures, based in Himara. We specialize exclusively in private boat tours, offering a more personalized and intimate experience tailored entirely to your preferences. Discover hidden beaches, crystal-clear waters, and charming coastal bays at your own pace.

Each journey is led by a highly experienced captain with extensive knowledge of the coastline, ensuring not only your safety but also insider access to the most beautiful and secluded spots along the Ionian Sea. Whether you’re seeking relaxation, swimming in untouched bays, or a touch of adventure, every tour is designed to create unforgettable moments.

With flexibility, comfort, and attention to detail at the core of what we do, Bayamar Adventures promises a unique and scenic escape along one of the Mediterranean’s most stunning coastlines.

How private is the tour? ▼
Completely. We never combine separate groups. The entire boat and Captain are reserved exclusively for you and your chosen companions.
Payment and Booking? ▼
Bookings are handled efficiently via WhatsApp to ensure clear communication and date availability. Simply choose your tour and send us your details!
Do you provide drinks? ▼
Yes, a cooler with fresh water is provided. You are also welcome to bring your own favorite drinks and snacks to enjoy on board.
Is there a toilet on board? ▼
To maintain the agility needed to explore small sea caves, our boat does not have a toilet. We recommend using the marina facilities before we depart.
What if the weather is bad? ▼
If the captain determines that sea conditions are unsafe, we will first attempt to reschedule. If that's not possible, a full refund will be provided.
Can we play our own music? ▼
Yes! Our boat is equipped with a high-quality Bluetooth sound system so you can set the perfect mood for your private escape.
